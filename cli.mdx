Experience the next generation of cloud management where artificial intelligence meets developer productivity.
xCommand works locally or remotely, as you keep interacting xCommand starts to build context and memories 
about you and your platform.

Plugins allow xCommand to interact with users or different systems. Out of the box, you can interact with
your xCommand agents from Slack, Teams, etc. xCommand will be able to connect to your PagerDuty, Incident.io, Linear, Notion
with the help of plugins.

Run xCommand as a server, adds the further capabilities to your agent, why babysit your agent when it can manage your worklife
and platform. That allows xCommand to be your 24/7 on-call operator when needed.
