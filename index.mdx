xCommand is a next generation AI-powered cloud manager that helps
engineers to build and manage the cloud platforms at scale.
We combine the power of AI with deep infrastructure expertise to deliver 
solutions that scale with your business.

Our powerful CLI works within your organisation, with the power and
flexibility to be used as a standalone agent or as a remote agent deployed
as a server. xCommand comes with environment aware high spec
engineering capabilities, to combine and to amplify the power of human 
engineering.

xCommand comes with a huge knowledge base and wide range of tools to help you build and
manage platforms across multiple cloud providers. A variety of plugins 
to let your agents work and channel the thoughts with humans or other agents.
We are different, we want to make the world a better place. While others help
you build and write the applications, we help you deploy and manage them and many more.
